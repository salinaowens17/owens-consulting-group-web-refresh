## Owens Consulting Group — Website Redesign Prototype

A clean, professional, trustworthy refresh of owensconsulting.org. Same information, modernized layout and typography, organized into a streamlined multi-page structure.

### Visual style

- **Palette:** crisp white background, deep navy as primary, environmental green as accent, warm neutrals for surfaces
- **Type:** serif headings (credibility) paired with a clean Georgia font body
- **Feel:** generous whitespace, strong section hierarchy, subtle dividers, minimal imagery used purposefully (no stock-photo overload like the current site)
- **Trust signals up front:** TCEQ Provider #782 badge, TDLR-approved badge, years of service, contact info always visible
